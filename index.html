Background

I was born on the 8th of May and raised in the Greater Cleveland area of Ohio. Cleveland Rocks! My parents and brothers immigrated to the United States in 1952. One of my favorite childhood memories is going with my family every Saturday to shop at the West Side Market, visit Cleveland Pubic Library, and, finally, stop for a treat at Hough bakeries! My husband and I have been married over 20 years and raised three wonderful children.
